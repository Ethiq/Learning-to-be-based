# Day 2 — Why Base Over Other L2s?

## The Distribution Advantage

Most blockchains struggle to get users. Base has a massive advantage: **Coinbase already has 110+ million verified users.**

Those users can move to Base with minimal friction:

1. User buys crypto on Coinbase
2. Coinbase surfaces Base-native apps and features
3. User starts interacting with Web3 — often without even realizing it

So Base becomes the gateway from **Web2 → Web3**.

## Why This Is a Big Deal

Other L2s (Arbitrum, Optimism, zkSync) have to spend millions on incentive programs, airdrops, and marketing just to get users to bridge over and try their chain.

Base skips all of that. Coinbase *is* the onramp.

If you've got a Coinbase account, you're one click away from being on Base. No separate wallet setup, no bridge transactions, no seed phrase management for new users.

## What It Means for Builders

Building on Base means your app is potentially accessible to the largest verified user base in crypto. That distribution channel is hard to replicate.

## Key Takeaway

In crypto, distribution is everything. Base has more of it than any other L2 — and it's not even close.
